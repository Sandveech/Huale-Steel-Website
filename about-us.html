INTRODUCTION

Originating from the steel trading business, with a strong track record in exporting and importing steel between Cambodia and China. Leveraging the close diplomatic and economic ties between the governments of the two countries, as well as Cambodia's unique geographical position and growing economic potential, the Board of Directors made the strategic decision to establish HUALE STEEL. Located at the hear of one of Cambodia's dynamic ecnomic zones, HUALE STEEL is positioned to contribute to the nation's industrial growth while serving as a bridge for international trade and development.

VISION

Our plan of action is to continually participate in extending our generation limits and increasing our production capabilities with the explicit goal of meeting the epidemically growing global demand and therefore maintaining our position at the leading edge of the steel industry.

MISSION

Our objective is to manufacture and offer the highest quality products to our clients while using sustainable techniques that adhere to the highest worldwide environmental control requirements. We believe that profoundly skilled and driven workers are the key to achieving our goals, and we will continue to provide excellent training and investment in their growth.
